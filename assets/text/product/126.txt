Xiaomi Redmi Note 9 comes with 6.53 inches Full HD+ IPS LCD screen. It has a left punch-hole design on the front. The display is protected by strong 5th generation Gorilla Glass. The back camera is of quad 48+8+2+2 MP with PDAF, ultrawide, depth sensor, dedicated macro camera etc. and Full HD video recording. The front camera is of 13 MP. Xiaomi Redmi Note 9 comes with 5020 mAh big battery with a 18W Fast Charging solution. It has 3 or 4 GB RAM, 2.0 GHz octa-core CPU and Mali-G52 MC2 GPU. It is powered by a MediaTek Helio G85 (12 nm) chipset. The phone comes with 64 or 128 GB internal storage and dedicated MicroSD slot. There is a back-mounted fingerprint sensor in this phone.
